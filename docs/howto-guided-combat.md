# Guided Combat HOWTO

1. **Enable Interaction Guides**
   - Open the match info popup in an online game and toggle *Interaction Guides* on (the setting persists locally).
2. **Trigger an attack prompt**
   - Move one of your untapped units with base power onto an enemy-occupied tile. Confirm that the Move vs Move & Attack dialog appears only when valid targets exist.
3. **Declare and resolve**
   - Choose *Move & Attack*. Verify the defender sees the new panel, can select/deselect units or the avatar, and that clicking *Resolve Attack* taps the participants and writes a single combat log entry on both clients.
4. **Cancel flow**
   - Repeat and click *Cancel* in the defense panel; confirm the combat state clears for both players.
5. **Guides off regression**
   - Disable the toggle and move units again to ensure no prompts or panels appear and movement behaves exactly as before.
